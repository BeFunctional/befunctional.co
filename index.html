About Functional

Character and craftsmanship is often overlooked in our fast-paced modern lives. Functional is a community exploring cultural history and connecting with the passionate individuals pursuing their craft who choose quality and character.

Our goal is to find that character, craftsmanship, and virtue in tools and products being created today, while also exploring the ideologies associated with what it means to be Functional.

Join us as we explore these concepts and pursue a more functional future.
