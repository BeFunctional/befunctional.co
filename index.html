About Functional Gent

Character and craftsmanship is often overlooked in our fast-paced modern lives. Functional Gent is an initiative exploring cultural history and connecting with the passionate individuals pursuing their craft who choose quality and character.

Our goal is to find that character, craftsmanship, and virtue in tools and products being created today, while also exploring the ideologies associated with what it means to be a Functional Gent.

Join us on our journey as we explore these concepts and pursue a more functional future.
